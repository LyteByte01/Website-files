Who are we?

LyteByte started in mid 2020 with the name of thattechguruu as a 1 man personal project called thattechguruu, this included a very poor HTML and CSS website with a half functioning contact form, 2 tech articles, and a very bad coding style. Thattechguruu was then hosted on infinityfree with a free included subdomain at thattechguruu.epizy.com and the first version came out in june of 2021.

The website then sat dormant for the next 8 months and had no updates whatsoever. and in the late end of january 2022 it was decided for the website to be taken down and the whole project just be started over. This is when 2 more develpers were added to the team as an opportunity for them to learn web devlopment and then you get to today, a three man group doing what they love

What do we do?

Well, that is a good question, when LyteByte was first conceptualised, there was no clear concept or idea we just know we wanted to have fun. So we decided to not stick to one clear path and just do what we want, this is why there is such a diverse range of content on this website.
